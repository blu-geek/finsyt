Executive Overview

Built for compliance mandates.
Designed for scale.

FinSyt is a web-based, production-deployed financial intelligence platform that goes far beyond KYC. It delivers end-to-end visibility into the entire customer financial lifecycle — from first onboarding through ongoing account behaviour, transaction monitoring, anomaly detection, industry-level performance benchmarking, and ecosystem-wide loan scoring via BVN linkage.

Built natively for African financial services, FinSyt addresses the compliance mandate at the point of onboarding while simultaneously powering the operational intelligence institutions need post-onboarding: account turnover analysis, transaction volume tracking, overdrawn account flags, unusual deposit or withdrawal alerts, and an interactive admin dashboard consolidating all signals into a single command centre.

"FinSyt does not just onboard a customer — it monitors, scores, and flags their financial behaviour across the entire institution and, via BVN, across the wider lending ecosystem."

The Problem

A $4.2B compliance burden --
and a $12B blind spot.

Financial institutions in Nigeria and across Africa face a compounding crisis -- not just at the point of onboarding, but throughout the entire customer financial lifecycle. The cost is borne in time, fraud losses, bad debt, and regulatory exposure.

⏱
Slow, Manual Onboarding
Traditional KYC processes require physical branch visits, manual document checks, and 3–7 day verification cycles. Customers abandon. Revenue is lost. Compliance risk grows.
⚠️
Inadequate Fraud Detection
Duplicate BVNs, synthetic identities, and suspicious transaction patterns go undetected in manual review workflows. The average Nigerian bank loses millions annually to identity fraud.
📉
Blunt Credit Scoring
Without structured risk data, lending decisions default to collateral-only underwriting — excluding the 60%+ of creditworthy Nigerians with thin or no credit file histories.
🏛
Regulatory Mandate, No Tools
CBN and NDPR compliance requirements for KYC and data protection are non-negotiable for licensed institutions — yet most lack affordable, purpose-built tooling to meet them.
💸
High Cost of Enterprise Solutions
Global KYC platforms price at $50K–$500K+ annual contracts — entirely inaccessible for MFBs and mid-tier banks with lean IT budgets and limited procurement bandwidth.
🌍
Not Built for African Context
Western platforms lack support for Nigerian document types (BVN, Voter's Card, NIN), local identity APIs, Naira-denominated risk models, or understanding of informal income structures.
📊
No Post-Onboarding Intelligence
Most institutions onboard a customer then lose visibility. Account turnover trends, unusual transaction patterns, overdrawn statuses, and large unexplained movements go unmonitored until damage is done.
🔗
Fragmented Loan Visibility
Without BVN-linked cross-institution scoring, a borrower can hold active loans at multiple MFBs simultaneously — invisible to any single lender and undetectable until default materialises across the ecosystem.
The Solution

Eight modules. One platform.
Zero blind spots.

FinSyt delivers a unified, eight-module financial intelligence stack — from first customer touch through real-time transaction monitoring, behavioural anomaly detection, industry benchmarking, and ecosystem-wide loan performance scoring via BVN. Each module is equally deployable as a standalone siloed product for institutions with specific use-case requirements. Browse all eight modules below — use the navigation or wait for auto-advance.

MODULE A
Digital Onboarding & KYC
End-to-end customer onboarding with document capture, selfie video verification, and real-time status tracking — eliminating branch visits entirely. Available as a standalone siloed product for institutions requiring only digital KYC capability.
  • User registration & Supabase Auth login
  • Document upload: NIN, Passport, BVN, Voter's Card, Utility Bill
  • Selfie video capture with liveness detection
  • Real-time KYC submission status tracking
  • Secure audit trail for all submissions
  • API-driven verification via Smile Identity / Dojah
MODULE B
Transaction Monitoring & Account Turnover
Continuous tracking of account-level transaction activity — giving institutions a real-time view of financial health, volume trends, and turnover patterns per customer and across the portfolio.
  • Account turnover analysis: inflow vs outflow over rolling periods
  • Transaction volume tracking: daily, weekly, monthly aggregates
  • Balance trend visualisation per account
  • Period-over-period comparison for performance review
  • Drill-down from portfolio view to individual account level
  • Exportable transaction summaries for audit & compliance
MODULE C
Anomalous Account Activity Flagging
Automated, rule-driven detection of account behaviours that deviate from established norms — surfacing risk signals before they become losses, with instant tiered administrator alerts. Available as a standalone siloed product for post-onboarding account surveillance.
  • Overdrawn account status detection & immediate alert
  • Unusual deposit pattern flagging: velocity, source, size anomalies
  • Large withdrawal anomaly detection (institution-configurable threshold)
  • Dormant-to-active account reactivation flags
  • Rapid successive transaction sequencing alerts
  • Severity classification: Low / Medium / High / Critical
MODULE D
Industry-Based Activity & Performance Filtering
Segment, benchmark, and evaluate account performance by industry classification — enabling institutions to identify sector-level risk concentrations and high-performing customer cohorts.
  • Industry / sector tagging at account level
  • Cross-industry performance comparison dashboard
  • Sector-level transaction volume & turnover benchmarking
  • Risk concentration analysis by industry segment
  • Cohort filtering: sector, account type, risk tier combinations
  • Industry-specific scoring weight configuration per institution
MODULE E
Credit Risk Scoring Engine
A configurable, rule-based credit scoring engine generating structured risk categories — enabling data-driven lending decisions across the full credit file spectrum.
  • Rule-based scoring: transaction behaviour, income signals, turnover
  • Output: Risk Category (Low / Medium / High)
  • Institution-configurable scoring weights per customer segment
  • Score factor breakdown with per-decision audit trail
  • Score versioning for model governance & regulatory review
  • Extensible architecture: ML model integration-ready
MODULE F
Ecosystem-Wide Loan Performance & BVN Scoring
The deepest intelligence layer in FinSyt. BVN-anchored cross-institution loan performance visibility — exposing multi-lender exposure, repayment history, and ecosystem risk invisible to single-institution views. Available as a standalone siloed product for lending portfolio intelligence.
  • BVN-anchored customer identity linkage across institutions
  • Cross-institution active loan exposure detection (stacking risk)
  • Repayment performance scoring: on-time, late, default history
  • Ecosystem-level borrower risk score output
  • NPL (Non-Performing Loan) early warning signal generation
  • Network effect: scoring accuracy improves as ecosystem grows
MODULE G
Fraud Detection & Identity Alert System
Real-time fraud signal detection using pre-defined identity and behavioural rules — with instant tiered alerts, a structured remediation workflow, and cross-module fraud signal correlation. Available as a standalone siloed product for dedicated fraud and identity intelligence.
  • Duplicate BVN & National ID detection across all submissions
  • Synthetic identity & suspicious data pattern flagging
  • Cross-module signal correlation: KYC + transaction + loan anomalies
  • Real-time admin alerts with Low / Medium / High / Critical tiering
  • Resolved vs. active alert queue with SLA tracking
  • Alert payload logging for regulatory & legal evidence packages
MODULE H · COMMAND CENTRE
Interactive Admin Dashboard — Evaluation & Monitoring
A unified, real-time command centre consolidating all seven intelligence modules into a single interactive administrative interface — built for decision-making speed, institutional oversight, and regulatory auditability. Institutions activating siloed modules receive a scoped dashboard view limited to their activated components.
  • Live KYC submission queue with status pipeline
  • One-click approve / reject with full audit logging
  • Role-based access: admin, reviewer, risk analyst
  • Institution-level configuration & API key management
  • Real-time anomaly & fraud alert feed across all modules
  • Account-level drill-down: KYC + transactions + score + loans
  • Industry segment performance heatmap & benchmarks
  • BVN ecosystem loan score per borrower record
  • Portfolio health: volume, turnover, NPL early warning rate
  • Exportable compliance & risk audit reports
  • All PII encrypted at rest & in transit (TLS)
  • NDPR-aligned · CBN KYC guideline-ready
User Architecture

Two principals.
One seamless system.

FinSyt operates across two distinct but tightly integrated user layers — the financial institution as the operator, and their end-customer as the applicant.

Institution User
Banks, MFBs & Lending Firms
The institutional operator configures, manages, and monitors the entire compliance workflow from a dedicated admin dashboard — with full control over KYC decisions, fraud alerts, and credit risk outputs.
  • Interactive admin dashboard — all eight modules unified
  • KYC approve / reject with one-click workflow
  • Real-time account turnover & transaction volume monitoring
  • Anomaly flag queue: overdrawn, unusual deposits, large withdrawals
  • Industry-segment performance filtering & benchmarking
  • BVN-linked ecosystem loan score per borrower
  • Credit risk score review, override & factor audit trail
  • Fraud & identity alert queue with SLA tracking
  • Exportable compliance & risk audit packages
End Customer
Account Applicants & Borrowers
The end customer navigates a guided, mobile-optimised onboarding experience — submitting KYC documents, completing selfie video verification, and tracking their application status in real time.
  • Guided account opening form flow
  • Document upload: NIN, BVN, Passport, Utility Bill
  • Selfie video capture for liveness verification
  • Real-time KYC application status tracking
  • Secure, encrypted document submission
  • Mobile-first experience — no branch visit required
